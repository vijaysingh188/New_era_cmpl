HEALTH PERIGON

Perigon means an angle of 360°, a full circle. Health needs an all-round, full 360°, approach to ensure holistic health. Good health goes beyond just the physical part but also includes social, emotional, spiritual and intellectual aspects.

Similarly, healthcare needs an all-round team approach, where various healthcare specialists along with family members and caretakers contribute to help individuals lead a healthy life.

At Health Perigon we strive to integrate the latest technological innovations in information and communication technology with updated medical science and offer a digital platform that caters to needs of all stakeholders in healthcare delivery. Keeping individual at the centre, with the aim of offering superior quality healthcare, this digital platform serves the needs of every healthcare delivery provider as well as patient caregiver and caretaker.

Artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms help dive deep in data and generate new insights with a special focus on preventive health. At Health Perigon, we take both health and technology seriously and hence we ensure not just compliance with the latest guidelines in healthcare but also latest security and privacy laws.

Commitment differentiates our team from the rest and we are driven by scientific evidence, ethical dealings and the spirit of innovation. We ensure compliance with all regulations, rules and laws, both in letter and spirit.

VISION MISSION

To be the world leader in integrating technological innovation with updated medical science to offer holistic, value-added, safe, affordable and accessible healthcare solutions to all stakeholders.

  • To continuously develop a data driven platform that focuses on all aspects of healthcare coupled with integration of latest technological innovations in information and communication technology.
  • To meaningfully use artificial intelligence and machine learning on the data generated and offer better healthcare solutions while maintaining highest levels of data privacy and security
  • To cater to the needs of varied healthcare providers and policy makers who can rely on the platform for its evidence based approach, ease-of-use and comprehensiveness.
  • To empower individuals, patients and caregivers who trust the platform for its patient-centric approach, authenticity, security and user-friendly interface.
